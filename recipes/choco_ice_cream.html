Chocolate Ice Cream Recipe

As adults and children, some of the most popular types of food seen across the world are chocolate and ice cream. Who doesn't love themselves some chocolate, am I right? Same thing with ice cream. Now, imagine combining them both? By following the recipe, you wont have to anymore!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 3 egg yolk, lightly beaten
  • 2 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Make sure to gather all the ingredients
  2. In a saucepan, combine the milk, sugar, cocoa powder, and salt together at medium-level heat. While stirring, be sure to being the saucepan to a simmer
  3. Inside of a small bowl, but some eggs yolks inside, before slowly whisking in a 1/2 cup fo hot milk inside. Make sure that the saucepan is heated up and stirred properly (DO NOT BOIL)
  4. After removing the saucepan from the heat, stir in somce chopped chocolate until it melts inside.
  5. Pour everything inside a chiiled-up bowl and refrigerate, while stirring occasionally, for approx. 2 hours.
  6. Take out the bowl and stir in some cream and vanilla; pour into the ice cream maker and freeze it accordingly

And voila! You have yourself a chocolate ice cream that you or an entire family can enjoy!
