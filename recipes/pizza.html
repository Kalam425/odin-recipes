Pizza Homemade Recipe

Homemade pizza has a very authentic satisfaction in producing, for most people anyway. Why go out and order a pie, when you can feel like a chef at home and make your own order correctly too? Besides, while it's heating up in the oven, you can relax on your couch instead of waiting in line.

That is why if you want the best flavored pizza, look no further than your own kitchen and this page! Here we have some of the best recipe that anyone can confidently follow and perfect enough like any culinarian.......

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 Â½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. After you gather all the ingredients, make sure to preheat the oven to 450 degrees F or 230 degrees C before then greasing the pizza pan lightly.
  2. Pour the warm water inside a bowl before adding the yeast and sugar inside it; For 10 minutes, gently mix the bowl until it becomes creamy.
  3. With either your hands or a dough hook, be sure to add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; Surface must be smooth once properly beat.
  4. Leave the bowl the way it is for about 5 minutes.
  5. Take the mixture-turned dough out and flatten it into 12 inch circle with the pat and roll method.
  6. Place the flattened dough on the pizza pan
  7. While placed on top, even and spread out the crust while adding any sauce or toppings of your choice
  8. Make sure to bake the pizza in the preheated oven for approximately 15-20 minutes. Doing that while give the pizza that golden brown color.
  9. Take the freshly-baked pizza out of the oven and let it sit for about 5 minutes; After that, voila and enjoy!
